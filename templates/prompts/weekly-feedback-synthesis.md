# Weekly Feedback Synthesis

## Goal

Convert raw feedback into prioritized product signals.

Bias the synthesis toward: `{{businessFocus}}`.

## Inputs

- support notes
- sales notes
- analytics changes
- customer requests
- repeated complaints
- positive patterns

## Expected Output

- top themes
- frequency signals
- business impact
- suggested product actions
- items to ignore or defer

## Coordination

- `feedback-synthesizer` consolidates
- `product-manager` ranks
- `project-shepherd` routes backlog follow-up
