# Sales To Product Handoff Workflow

## Trigger

Use when sales feedback should influence roadmap or product design.

## Primary Agents

- `account-strategist`
- `sales-proposal-strategist`
- `product-manager`

## Steps

1. Capture the customer need and deal context.
2. Separate must-haves from nice-to-haves.
3. Translate requests into product language.
4. Flag promise risk or scope creep.
5. Route valid input into the product process.

## Exit Criteria

- sales feedback is normalized
- product risk is explicit
- unsupported promises do not carry forward
