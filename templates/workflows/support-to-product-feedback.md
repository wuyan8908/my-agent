# Support To Product Feedback Workflow

## Trigger

Use when recurring support issues should become product input.

## Primary Agents

- `support-responder`
- `feedback-synthesizer`
- `product-manager`

## Steps

1. Classify the support issue.
2. Group it with similar reports.
3. Estimate impact and frequency.
4. Decide whether it becomes a product task.
5. Route it to the right backlog owner.

## Exit Criteria

- the issue is summarized as a product signal
- duplicate reports are merged
- the next action is clear
